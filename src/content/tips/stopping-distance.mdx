**Reaction** = (speed ÷ 10) × 3 metres · **Braking** = (speed ÷ 10)² metres · **Stopping** = both added · **Emergency stop** = braking ÷ 2.

> At 50 km/h: 15 + 25 = **40 m** to stop (≈20 m in an emergency).
