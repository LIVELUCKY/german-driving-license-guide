---

Turn your **head visibly** before every pull-away, lane change and turn. Examiners watch specifically for the head movement — not just the mirrors.

**Mirror → Blind spot → Signal → Go** — in that order, every time.

Missing the Schulterblick is the **single most common** reason for failing the practical exam.
